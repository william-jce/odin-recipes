Baked Salmon with Kale

Baked salmon with kale is a simple dish requiring very little preparation and effort. A perfect dinner after a busy and tiring day.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • ~225g salmon fillet
  • 1/2 tbs lemon juice
  • 1 sprig of dill chopped
  • 200g of kale leaves stripped and torn
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 200g of brown rice and quinoa, pre-cooked

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160C/fan 140C.
  2. Coat large piece of foil with oil.
  3. Place salmon in the centre, pour over lemon juice and scatter over dill.
  4. Add kale and season the parcel with salt and pepper.
  5. Bring sides of the foil together to seal the foil, and bake for 15-20 minutes or until salmon flakes apart (don't overcook!).
  6. Serve rice, salmon, and kale and drizzle over any juices.
